Avatar

Avatar is used to represent the users profile picture.

Square Avatar

Square Avatar has 5 different sizes you can use avatar-sm for small image, avatar-md for medium, avatar-lg for large and so onn. 

                         <figure>
                            <img src="/assests/avatar.jpg" alt="" class="avatar-sm m-4">
                        </figure>
                        <figure>
                            <img src="/assests/avatar.jpg" alt="" class="avatar-md m-4">
                        </figure>
                        <figure>
                            <img src="/assests/avatar.jpg" alt="" class="avatar-lg m-4">
                        </figure>
                        <figure>
                            <img src="/assests/avatar.jpg" alt="" class="avatar-xl m-4">
                        </figure>
                        <figure>
                            <img src="/assests/avatar.jpg" alt="" class="avatar-xxl m-4">
                        </figure>

Round Avatar

Round Avatar has also 5 sizes.To make the square avatar to round you can use image-round class in image tag.

                        <figure>
                            <img src="/assests/avatar.jpg" alt="" class="avatar-sm image-round m-4">
                        </figure>
